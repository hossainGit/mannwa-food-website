About Mannwa

We began exporting to the USA in 2018, and are now expanding our reach to buyers across the UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Italy, South Korea, France, Dubai, and beyond. Our product range includes cookies, toast biscuits, flour-based snacks, frozen vegetables, and various types of meatballs and singara made from halal green chicken. We also offer high-quality flour-based products such as paratha, pastry sheets, dal puri, aloo puri, spring rolls, and roti — all sourced directly from our agricultural farms to ensure freshness, food safety, and premium quality for our consumers.
